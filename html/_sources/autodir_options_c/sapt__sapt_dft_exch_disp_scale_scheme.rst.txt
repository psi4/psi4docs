:term:`SAPT_DFT_EXCH_DISP_SCALE_SCHEME <SAPT_DFT_EXCH_DISP_SCALE_SCHEME (SAPT)>`
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      Scheme for approximating exchange-dispersion for SAPT-DFT. ``NONE`` Use unscaled ``Exch-Disp2,u`` . ``FIXED`` Use a fixed factor :term:`SAPT_DFT_EXCH_DISP_FIXED_SCALE <SAPT_DFT_EXCH_DISP_FIXED_SCALE (SAPT)>` to scale ``Exch-Disp2,u`` . ``DISP`` Use the ratio of ``Disp2,r`` and ``Disp2,u`` to scale ``Exch-Disp2,u`` .

      * **Type**: string
      * **Possible Values**: NONE, FIXED, DISP
      * **Default**: DISP
