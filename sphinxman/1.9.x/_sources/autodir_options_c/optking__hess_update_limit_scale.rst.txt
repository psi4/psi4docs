:term:`HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT_SCALE <HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT_SCALE (OPTKING)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      If :term:`HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT <HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT (OPTKING)>` is true, changes to the Hessian from the update are limited to the larger of HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT_SCALE * (the previous value) and :term:`HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT_MAX <HESS_UPDATE_LIMIT_MAX (OPTKING)>` [au].

      * **Type**: double
      * **Default**: 0.50
