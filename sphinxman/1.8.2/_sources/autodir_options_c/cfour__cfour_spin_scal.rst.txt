:term:`CFOUR_SPIN_SCAL <CFOUR_SPIN_SCAL (CFOUR)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      ON (=1) requests the spin-component scaled variant of the MP2 approach. This keyword has only an effect when :term:`CFOUR_CALC_LEVEL <CFOUR_CALC_LEVEL (CFOUR)>` =MP2 is specified and must be used together with :term:`CFOUR_REFERENCE <CFOUR_REFERENCE (CFOUR)>` =UHF.

      * **Type**: :ref:`boolean <op_c_boolean>`
      * **Default**: false
