:term:`VAL_EX_LEVEL <VAL_EX_LEVEL (DETCI)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      In a RAS CI, this is the additional excitation level for allowing electrons out of RAS I into RAS II. The maximum number of holes in RAS I is therefore :term:`EX_LEVEL <EX_LEVEL (DETCI)>` + VAL_EX_LEVEL.

      * **Type**: integer
      * **Default**: 0
