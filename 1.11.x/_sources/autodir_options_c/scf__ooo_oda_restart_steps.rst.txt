:term:`OOO_ODA_RESTART_STEPS <OOO_ODA_RESTART_STEPS (SCF)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      For :term:`ORBITAL_OPTIMIZER_PACKAGE <ORBITAL_OPTIMIZER_PACKAGE (GLOBALS)>` = `OOO`, restart ODA steps when DIIS hasn't improved energy for this many SCF iterations. This option is temporary.

      * **Type**: integer
      * **Default**: 10
