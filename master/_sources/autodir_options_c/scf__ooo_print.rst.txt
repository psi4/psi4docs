:term:`OOO_PRINT <OOO_PRINT (SCF)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      For :term:`ORBITAL_OPTIMIZER_PACKAGE <ORBITAL_OPTIMIZER_PACKAGE (GLOBALS)>` = `OOO`, verbosity of printing to screen. 0 prints nothing. 1 prints one line per iter (note that RHF rms(density) printed differs by half from convergence criterion. 5 is common and adds occupancy printing. 12 is max.
