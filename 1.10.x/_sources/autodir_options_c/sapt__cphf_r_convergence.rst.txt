:term:`CPHF_R_CONVERGENCE <CPHF_R_CONVERGENCE (SAPT)>`
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      Convergence criterion for residual of the CPHF/CPKS coefficients in the SAPT :math:`E_{ind,resp}^{(20)}` term. This applies to wavefunction-based SAPT or SAPT(DFT). See :term:`CPHF_R_CONVERGENCE <CPHF_R_CONVERGENCE (FISAPT)>` for fragment-partitioned or intramolecular SAPT.

      * **Type**: :ref:`conv double <op_c_conv>`
      * **Default**: 1e-8
