:term:`DF_BASIS_F12 <DF_BASIS_F12 (F12)>`
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      Choose a density-fitting basis for integrals. For :term:`MP2_TYPE <MP2_TYPE (F12)>` =DF, this is applied to non-F12 SCF and MP2 parts, too, regardless of :term:`DF_BASIS_SCF <DF_BASIS_SCF (SCF)>` or :term:`DF_BASIS_MP2 <DF_BASIS_MP2 (DFMP2)>` .

      * **Type**: string
      * **Possible Values**: :ref:`basis string <apdx:basisElement>`
      * **Default**: No Default
